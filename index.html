Process

Six steps. Zero surprises.

1
Free Home Visit

We walk your home with you, every wall, every corner. No charge, no obligation.

2
Requirement Gathering

How you live, what you store, what mornings look like. We listen before we sketch.

3
Material Approval

Every board, laminate and hinge in your hands before a single panel is cut.

4
Execution

Our own craftsmen build on site, no middlemen, no drift from the drawings.

5
Quality Check

Every edge, alignment and soft-close checked against the approved design.

6
Delivery

Keys back in 25–30 days. Your move-in date is a promise, not an estimate.
